# Warm-Up Rhythm Guide — Stay Human, Stay Clean

## Why This Matters
Google and Microsoft spam filters flag accounts that send emails in bulk, at odd hours,
or with identical content. Mimicking human behavior keeps your domain reputation healthy.

---

## 1. The Daily Schedule (Gap Rule)

| Window | Time | Emails |
|--------|------|--------|
| Morning | 09:00 – 11:00 | 4 emails |
| Lunch break | 11:00 – 13:00 | — pause — |
| Early Afternoon | 13:00 – 15:00 | 6 emails |
| Short break | 15:00 – 16:00 | — pause — |
| Late Afternoon | 16:00 – 17:30 | 5 emails |

> Never send all emails at once. Spread them in clusters with real gaps between them.

---

## 2. Time Between Individual Sends

- Wait **3–5 minutes** between each email — even within the same cluster
- Use the waiting time to research the next lead, check the name spelling, or personalize the body
- This slow pace is exactly what mail servers look for to confirm you are a real person

---

## 3. Respect the Recipient's Time Zone

| Region | Time Zone | Send Window (local) | Stop By (Bucharest time) |
|--------|-----------|---------------------|--------------------------|
| Denmark, Norway, Switzerland | CET (UTC+1) | 09:00 – 16:00 CET | 17:00 Bucharest |
| UAE / Dubai | GST (UTC+4) | 09:00 – 16:00 GST | 15:00 Bucharest |

> UAE is 3 hours ahead — after 15:00 Bucharest time they are already leaving the office.

---

## 4. Variation is Key

- **Subject lines** — never use the exact same subject for all emails. Rotate at least 3 variations
- **Personalization** — always verify Name and Location are correct in each email
- Identical emails sent to multiple addresses are flagged automatically by spam filters

---

## 5. Monitor Your Bounce Rate

- If you receive an **"Address not found"** bounce → stop sending for that hour
- Too many bounces in a short window is a major red flag during warm-up
- Keep bounces below 2% of total sends

## 8. Quick Checklist Before Each Send

- [ ] Is it within the correct send window for this recipient's time zone?
- [ ] Has it been at least 3 minutes since the last email?
- [ ] Is the subject line different from the previous one?
- [ ] Is the recipient name and city correct in the body?
- [ ] Did the last email bounce? (if yes — wait 1 hour before next send)
